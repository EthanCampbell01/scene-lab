# Manual Scene Rating Rubric (0–5)

For each generated scene, score:

- coherence (0–5): internal consistency with state/facts; no contradictions
- pacing (0–5): escalation and rhythm; no bloated or repetitive beats
- agency (0–5): choices feel distinct and meaningful
- interrogationRealism (0–5): tactics feel plausible for interrogation/negotiation
- variantStrength (0–5): voice/theme present without breaking logic

Also record:
- pipeline (topDown/bottomUp)
- variantId
- sceneId
- notes (bullets)
